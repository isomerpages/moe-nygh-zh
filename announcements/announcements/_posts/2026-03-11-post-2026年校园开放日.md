<h4><strong>Join us at our Open House on Saturday, 11 April, from 8am to 2pm!</strong></h4>
<p>Thinking about your next big step in 2027? Come discover what makes Nanyang Girls' High School a place where students learn, grow, and thrive.</p>
<p>Explore our innovative academic programmes at the Curriculum Showcase, and experience the vibrant student life at our CCA Showcase, featuring concerts and interactive booths hosted by our student groups.</p>
<p>Attend school talks and live Q&amp;A sessions with our Principal and Vice-Principals, where you can learn more about our vision, programmes, and student experience.</p>
<p>Embark on a fun campus trail! Participate in hands-on activities, and engage with our school leaders, teachers, parents, and students. </p>
<p>Whether you are looking for a school that helps you grow, inspires you to lead, and welcomes you into a strong community, we warmly invite you to experience the spirit of Nanyang Girls' High School.
</p>

<br><strong>Location:</strong> 2 Linden Drive 288683, Nanyang Girls'
